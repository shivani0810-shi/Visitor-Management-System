How It Works

A simple digital workflow designed to make visitor handling faster, safer, and easier for every organization.

1
Register Visitor

Visitor details are entered into the VisiTrack system.

2
Employee Approval

The concerned employee reviews and approves the visit request.

3
Generate QR Pass

A secure QR pass is generated after visitor approval.

4
Visitor Check-In

The visitor scans the QR pass and checks in at reception.

5
Visitor Check-Out

The checkout time is recorded when the visitor leaves.

6
Analytics & Reports

Admins can view reports and visitor history at any time.
